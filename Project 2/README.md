# DecodeLabs Project 2 – Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the DecodeLabs Data Analytics Internship Program. The objective was to perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the Sample Superstore dataset using Microsoft Excel to uncover business insights, identify trends, detect profitability issues, and provide data-driven recommendations.

---

## Dataset Information

* **Dataset:** Sample Superstore
* **Tool:** Microsoft Excel
* **Records:** 9,994
* **Variables:** 21
* **Analysis Type:** Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## Key Insights

* Technology generated the highest sales and overall profit.
* Furniture generated strong sales but comparatively low profitability.
* Tables and Bookcases were identified as major contributors to reduced profitability.
* The West region recorded the highest sales and profit.
* The Consumer segment generated the largest share of total sales.
* Sales and profits showed consistent growth throughout the analysis period.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Continue investing in high-performing Technology products.
* Review pricing and discount strategies for low-profit Furniture products.
* Investigate the causes of poor profitability in Tables and Bookcases.
* Strengthen growth strategies in lower-performing regions.
* Monitor profitability alongside sales to improve decision-making.
